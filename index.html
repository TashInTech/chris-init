Delivery timelines at Chris Royal depend on your location. For customers within Mbarara City and its immediate suburbs, we offer same-day delivery for orders placed before 2:00 PM East Africa Time. For customers located in nearby towns such as Bushenyi, Kabale, Ntungamo, and Ibanda, delivery typically takes 1 to 2 business days. For orders being shipped to Kampala and other distant regions of Uganda, the estimated delivery window is 2 to 3 business days. We coordinate all deliveries through trusted courier partners and provide order tracking details upon dispatch.
